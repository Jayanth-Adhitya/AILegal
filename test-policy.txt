Company Legal Policy - General Contract Requirements

1. Payment Terms
All contracts must specify payment terms of Net 30 days or less. Payment terms exceeding 30 days require executive approval.

2. Liability Limitations
Liability caps should not exceed the total contract value. Unlimited liability clauses are prohibited without legal review.

3. Termination Clauses
All contracts must include termination rights with at least 30 days notice period.

4. Intellectual Property
Company retains all intellectual property rights to work products created under the contract.

5. Confidentiality
All contracts must include mutual confidentiality obligations with a minimum 2-year term.

6. Dispute Resolution
Disputes should be resolved through arbitration in accordance with company jurisdiction.

7. Force Majeure
Contracts should include force majeure provisions protecting against unforeseeable circumstances.

8. Indemnification
Indemnification clauses must be mutual and reasonable in scope.
